What is a standard website?

A standard website includes up to 3 AI-generated pages, a responsive layout, SEO metadata, and one quality pass.

Additional pages, revisions, research actions, SEO packs, and premium design passes use AI credits — so you can go deeper on the projects that need it without paying for capacity you don\u2019t.

Pricing FAQ

Pricing, credits, and plan questions.

Each plan includes a set number of standard sites and a monthly pool of AI credits. A standard site (up to 3 pages, responsive, SEO metadata, one QA pass) uses about 10 credits. Credits also power extra pages, revisions, research actions, SEO packs, and premium design passes.
No. Team and user seats are sold as a paid add-on on every plan rather than bundled seat limits, so you only pay for the seats you actually use as your team grows.
Yes. Source-code export is included on every plan, including Starter — so you can hand off clean code or host the site with ApexSites from day one.
Hosting is included on Mid-Sized Agency. On Starter and Agency, hosting is available as an add-on. Hosting is designed as a recurring revenue stream you can resell to clients.
Monthly plan credits reset each month. Purchased credit packs can carry a longer expiration (up to 12 months), giving you flexibility for busy periods without undercutting your subscription.
Add a credit pack for a short-term spike, or upgrade to a higher plan for more included sites and credits. Enterprise offers fully custom site and credit volume.
Enterprise is for resellers, franchises, platforms, and high-volume teams that need custom volume, a custom white-label portal, SSO, dedicated support, an SLA, and negotiated usage.
Yes. The Agency plan adds white-label branding and Mid-Sized Agency adds advanced white-label — deliver everything under your own brand, domain, and client-facing settings.
